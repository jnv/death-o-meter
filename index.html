About the project

What happens when a data analyst and a programmer don’t have any plans for the New Year’s Eve? A private hackathon!

We gathered data from Wikidata.org, refined them, argued about how to visualise them, argued few times more and then finally visualised them.Both of us know that this is just the first step and we are planning to take more of them. In future, we would like to add some new cathegories of dead people (directors, polititians, maybe even sports persons), as well as extend the informational potential of the vizualization by adding causes of death and maybe visualise also those. Maybe, we will focus just on our country and make something like a local mutation with local data.

Actually, you can help us to do so. Here you can help us with the “cause of death” labeling and here you can send us your feedback.
